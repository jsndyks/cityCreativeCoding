Students Opting Out?

Now, a couple of colleagues have reported that students have "chosen to opt out of creative coding".

Let's be clear here - the submission is optional, but engagement and attendance are expected. Attendance is monitored (unreliably, but let's not emphasize that) and #CreativeCoding and the tutorials are an important part of the stage 1 educational provision.

Please ask your students to attend all lectures and engage in all activities and in particular ask them to consider the carefully curated What's The Point? document as they decide how much effort to put into the various learning opportunities that we provide.

Tutors Opting Out?

And a couple of students have told me that their personal tutors have not been in touch at all, despite our promise to connect students with tutors in induction week.
I am sure there are good reasons for this, but I am fielding enquiries and this is becoming disruptive. Please do get in touch as requested by Dhawal and documented on the Year 1 Personal Tutorials & #CreativeCoding page where the Tips for Engagement section is a good one to read. You can find the names of your tutees on the year-on-year multiple winner of the World's Worst Information System award - e:Vision.
