This project involves creating new ways to tell the time. You might create a fancy clock face or a completely original way to indicate time with colour or sound. There are infinite possibilities.

Processing has a number of functions that will report the current date and time:

  • year() Current year
  • month() Month of the year (1-12)
  • day() Day of the month (1-31)
  • hour() Hour of the day (0-23)
  • minute() Minute of the hour (0-59)
  • second() Second of the hour (0-59)

Can you use some or all of these to create your own clock? Perhaps a digital clock using fancy fonts or an analogue clock with hour, minute and second hands? Or what about a combination of the two? What would a 'digilogue' clock that combined numbers with hands look like?

Try to think creatively. What fresh ways are there of showing the time? If you couldn't use numbers or hand position, how could you show time? What would a flower clock look like? What would a clock look like that used actual 'hands'? Could you use draw a sun moving across the sky, or water filling a jar? If you had to design a 'gadget watch' for James Bond what would it do?

Or perhaps more something more abstract that uses colour, shape or sound to indicate the passage of time. See how creative you can be in coming up with your own design.
