This project involves recreating an early video game with Processing. The early video games were programmed on computers with a tiny fraction of the computing power of even the slowest laptop today, so you should no problem making a game work smoothly.

The idea behind this project is for you to choose a first or second generation video game and see if you can recreate it in Processing. The choice of game is up to you, but you may find the earlier games easier to recreate. If you are having trouble finding a suitable game, some early games to consider are listed below in rough order of sophistication.

  • Pong (two player bat and ball game)
  • Breakout (destroy wall with bat and ball)
  • Asteroids (destroy asteroids with rotating spaceship)
  • Centipede (blast away segments of an ever growing centipede)
  • Pacman (ghosts, pills and munching yellow thing running through a maze)

There are plenty of other games you might choose to build such as platform games, space invader style shooters etc. You could even make up your own.
