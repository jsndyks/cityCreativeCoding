A few of you have asked how to collect data and what we actually mean by data as you create your Dear Data inspired Data Diaries.

Basically, Data in this context is any recording made of an observation, and you should make these as easily as possible, with sufficient information to capture what has been observed. For example ...

You can collect numbers, photographs, words, anything you like really that captures the phenomenon that interests you.

Just do this regularly and with consistency, although what you actually collect may change over time as your data collection informs your process.

Try to minimise these changes though, as you really want your data to be as consistent over time as possible, but see in one of the examples below, that we decided to collect something additional as we realised that the data that we were capturing was not sufficiently rich
