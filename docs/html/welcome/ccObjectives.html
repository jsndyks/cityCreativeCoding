Module Objectives

We have designed the #Creative Coding module in order to achieve a series of specific objectives.

Knowing these and referring to them as you are learning should help us all achieve them.

Specifically, we hope to ...

  • introduce ways of learning at University - autonomous, experimental, reflective; lectures, classes, tutorials & Moodle
  • introduce key programming concepts
  • encourage creative thinking
  • encourage computational thinking
  • encourage students to get to know themselves and their peers through data, to reflect on their relationship with data
  • develop the Y1 Computer Science community
  • learn some core technology for productivity & collaboration and practice
  • build relationships between students and tutors

We want to do so ...

  • in a safe, supportive, penalty free, “free hit” environment
  • in which experimentation is encouraged, and you can ...
    • do something different, we are looking for individuality
    • be yourself, have fun, find your voice - “Finding Me”

We encourage you to reflect on these objectives as you learn and think about how you are doing, and let us know whether we are helping you achieve them.
