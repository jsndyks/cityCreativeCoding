Good luck with your _Dear Data Sketches_, try to enjoy experimenting a bit, and remember that we are not looking for particularly sophisticated work - just do something that makes you feel pleased. There are no marks to be gained, but good experience and connections to be made. And you get a social media badge!

One thing we have not emphasized sufficiently:

It is easy to have an IDEA, to know what your INTENTION is for the script, but to not be able to write the CODE to do it.

This can be frustrating with lots of blockages stopping you from getting to where you had hoped to arrive.

We don't have the time or energy to bang our head against frustrating blockages in this task.

#CreativeCoding and Processing work best if you have an idea, see what happens when you code, and go where the code takes you! This may be somewhere surprising, and not where you imagined in the first place! It may be interesting, revealing, satisfying! Serendipity is key here.

Try things out, follow your nose, and develop your ideas as to where you are going and what you are going to achieve - and make the most of what you learn on the way.

You might end up nowhere near where you thought you were going, but you may get somewhere interesting and learn a lot. That's the hope.

Take this attitude into your final week of _Dear Data_ coding as you work on your sketch.

I have added a few notes to the Creativity : Core materials that reflect this.
